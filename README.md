### About me

I'm a self-taught developer who ended up deep in Windows internals — kernel drivers, process manipulation, reverse engineering — mostly because I kept pulling on threads until I understood how things actually worked under the hood. No formal CS background, just interest-first learning.

I also build in Lua/Luau for Roblox, tinker with Minecraft client mods, and maintain a couple of personal sites.

- 🔭 Currently building tools around Windows internals and low-level performance work
- 🌱 Learning by taking things apart — reverse engineering as a study method, not just a hobby
- 🌐 Sites: [samoyed.sh](https://samoyed.sh) · [cherubim.solutions](https://cherubim.solutions)
- 🗣️ Trilingual: Swedish, English, Russian
